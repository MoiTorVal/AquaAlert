# Memorandum of Understanding (Template — informal pilot)

**Between:** [Resource Conservation District name] ("District")
**And:** Moises Valdez, AquaAlert ("Developer")
**Term:** [start date] – [end of growing season, date]

> Not a contract; a statement of mutual intent suitable as a grant exhibit.
> Have the District's usual reviewer look at it before signing.

## Purpose

Pilot AquaAlert — satellite-based crop water-stress alerts and water-savings
tracking — with 15–30 farmers in the District's service area for one growing
season, to reduce over-irrigation and simplify SGMA extraction reporting.

## Developer agrees to

1. Provide AquaAlert free to all pilot participants for the term.
2. Onboard farmers (English/Spanish) with the District's coordination.
3. Provide the District cohort-level (aggregate, anonymized) water savings,
   energy, and CO₂ figures monthly — never individual farm data without that
   farmer's written consent.
4. Generate SGMA extraction report exports for participating farmers.
5. Maintain participant data privacy: demographic self-ID data is voluntary
   and never shared; aggregates suppressed below 3 farms.

## District agrees to

1. Identify and introduce candidate farmers (target 15–30).
2. Provide a named point of contact for the pilot.
3. Review one mid-season and one end-of-season summary.
4. [Optional] Serve as fiscal sponsor on a joint USDA WETA application.

## Mutual understandings

- Either party may end participation with 30 days' notice.
- No funds change hands under this MOU.
- Farmers own their data and may leave the pilot at any time.
- Results may be cited in grant applications by either party.
